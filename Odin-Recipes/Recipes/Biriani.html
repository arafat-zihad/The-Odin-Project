Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Roshun
  • Water

Steps

  1. Marinate: Marinate meat (or vegetables) in yogurt and spices.
  2. Cook Rice: Partially cook basmati rice.
  3. Sauté: Sauté onions, ginger, garlic, and spices.
  4. Layer: Layer meat/vegetables and rice in a pot.
  5. Dum (Steam): Seal the pot and cook on low heat to steam.
  6. Garnish: Garnish with fried onions, cilantro, and mint.
