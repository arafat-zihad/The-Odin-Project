Moroccan Tajin

Tajines are simply delicious but in the most cases they're made with meat. Well, this one isn't and is just as falvourfull as the others. Unfortunately you need a tajine to make a tajine. And to heat up your tajine you will also need a gas stove. I guess there are also other options but this is the way I prepared my Tajine in Morocco.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Potatoes
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. First dice the onion and slice the vegetables.
  2. Add some oil to the bottom of the tajine but dn't cook it yet.
  3. Now you have to build a kind of tent with the vegetables over the tomatoes.
