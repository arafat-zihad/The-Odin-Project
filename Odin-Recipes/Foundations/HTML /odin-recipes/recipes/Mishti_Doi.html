Mishti Doi

Home

Description

A traditional Bengali dessert made by caramelizing sugar and blending it with thickened milk and yogurt.

Ingredients

  • Full cream milk
  • Sugar
  • Yogurt

Steps

  1. Boil milk until reduced to half.
  2. Caramelize sugar and mix into the milk.
  3. Cool slightly, then add yogurt and whisk.
  4. Pour into earthen pots.
  5. Set overnight until firm and chilled.
