Home

Description

Hilsa fish cooked in a pungent mustard paste, a signature Bengali delicacy often served with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • Hilsh fish pieces
  • Mustard seeds
  • Mustard oil
  • Green chilies
  • Turmeric powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Rub fish pieces with turmeric and salt, set aside.
  2. Heat mustard oil until smoking, then cool slightly.
  3. Add mustard paste, green chilies, and a little water.
  4. Place fish pieces gently into the gravy.
  5. Cover and simmer for 10–12 minutes until cooked.
  6. Serve hot with plain rice.
