Chicken Rezala

Home

Description

A Mughal-inspired mild chicken curry with yogurt, ghee, and aromatic spices — rich yet delicate.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Pieces
  • Yogurt
  • Ghee
  • Onion
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Cardamom, cinnamon, cloves
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, salt, and ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Heat ghee, add whole spices and onion paste.
  3. Add marinated chicken, cook until sealed.
  4. Simmer on low heat until chicken is tender.
  5. Garnish with fried onions and serve with paratha or pulao.
